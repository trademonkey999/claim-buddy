Source: Defence Services Travel Regulations, Edition 2025

Hotel/taxi/food ceilings are from TR 114A; rail class from TR 57, air class from TR 62. Hotel ceilings reflect the 25% DA-linked increase over base 7th-CPC values (₹9375/5625/2813/938/563). GST on hotel room tariff is added automatically by slab and is fully reimbursable on top of the ceiling: ≤₹1000 → 0% (exempt) · ₹1001–7500 → 5% (no ITC) · >₹7500 → 18% (ITC). Food is paid as a lump-sum DA (no vouchers), regulated by absence from HQ — <6h=30%, 6–12h=70%, >12h=100% (TR 114A.5). For Level 8 & below, hotel & taxi up to ceiling are payable on self-certification (no vouchers); Class-X city hotel ceiling is ₹1000/day on vouchers. Ceilings rise 25% whenever DA rises 50%. LTC (Ch.4) reimburses entitled-class fare for self & family only — no hotel/DA. Confirm every figure against the latest amendments before filing.
